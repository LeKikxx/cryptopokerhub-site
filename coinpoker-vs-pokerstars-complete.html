1. Rakeback Comparison

  • VIP level (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Supernova...)
  • Points earned per $1 rake
  • Monthly volume requirements
  • Bonuses available each month

Reality: Most recreational players get 5-15%. Only high-volume grinders reach 30-40%.

CoinPoker: Simple 33%

CoinPoker keeps it simple:

  • Everyone gets 33% rakeback
  • Paid every Monday automatically
  • No volume requirements
  • No tiers, no games, no complex calculations

Winner: CoinPoker for simplicity and consistency.

2. KYC and Privacy

PokerStars: Full Verification Required

To deposit and especially to withdraw:

  • Government-issued ID (passport, driver's license)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Proof of payment method
  • Sometimes selfie verification

Process takes 24-72 hours. Documents can be rejected for minor issues.

CoinPoker: Email + Crypto Only

To play on CoinPoker:

  • Valid email address
  • Crypto deposit (USDT, BTC, ETH, SOL)

That's it. No documents. No waiting. No verification.

Winner: CoinPoker for privacy and speed.
