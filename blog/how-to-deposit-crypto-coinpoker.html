Before You Deposit

Create your CoinPoker account if you haven't already. Registration takes just an email and password - no ID verification required.

Have your crypto ready in a wallet you control. Whether it's MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or an exchange wallet like Binance, make sure you can send transactions.

Double-check the deposit address before sending anything. Crypto transactions are irreversible. One wrong character and your funds could be lost forever.

Consider transaction fees before depositing. Network fees vary based on blockchain congestion. Sometimes it's worth waiting for fees to drop.

Step-by-Step Deposit Process

Step 1: Log in to your CoinPoker account and navigate to the Cashier or Deposit section.

Step 2: Select your preferred cryptocurrency from the list. USDT (TRC20 or ERC20) typically offers the best combination of low fees and fast confirmation.

Step 3: Copy the deposit address displayed. This is unique to your account for this specific transaction.

Step 4: Open your wallet or exchange and initiate a transfer to the address you copied. Paste carefully - don't type it manually.

Step 5: Wait for blockchain confirmations. USDT on TRC20 usually confirms in 1-2 minutes. BTC can take 10-60 minutes depending on network traffic.

Step 6: Your funds will appear in your CoinPoker account once confirmed. If there's a delay beyond expected time, contact support.

USDT Deposit (Recommended)

USDT is my recommended deposit method for poker players. Here's why:

No volatility risk - Your deposit stays worth the same whether you're winning or losing. No worrying about BTC crashing mid-session.

Low fees - USDT on TRC20 network costs pennies to transfer, even during busy periods.

Fast confirmation - TRC20 USDT confirms in about a minute on average.

To deposit USDT, select TRC20 (Tron) network for lowest fees, or ERC20 if your wallet only supports Ethereum. The process is identical otherwise.

BTC Deposit

Bitcoin deposits work the same way but require more confirmations for security. Expect 2-6 confirmations before your BTC appears in your account.

Network fees matter more with BTC - During congested periods, fees can spike to several dollars. Check a fee estimator before depositing small amounts.

Wait time is longer - A single Bitcoin block takes about 10 minutes on average. With 2-6 required confirmations, budget 20-60 minutes for your deposit to clear.

My recommendation: use USDT unless you specifically want to hold Bitcoin. The convenience and fee advantages are significant.

Pro Tips for Deposits

Test with a small amount first - Always send a test transaction before depositing your full bankroll. Verify the address works.

Time your deposits - Check blockchain congestion before depositing large amounts. Fees can vary 10x between busy and quiet periods.

Keep records - Screenshot your transaction IDs and deposit confirmations. The blockchain is transparent but your records help with accounting.

Buy CHP for rakeback - Deposit some USDT to play and some CHP to hold. The 33% rakeback pays for the CHP purchase quickly.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Deposit not showing up: Check blockchain explorers like Etherscan or Tronscan to verify the transaction was confirmed. If confirmed on blockchain, contact CoinPoker support with your transaction ID.

Wrong address used: If you sent BTC to an ETH address or vice versa, recovery is unlikely. Always double-check the network type before sending.

Minimum deposit not met: Each crypto has a minimum deposit amount. Deposits below minimum may be lost. Check the current minimums before sending.
