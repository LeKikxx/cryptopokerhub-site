What is Crypto Poker?

Crypto poker is simply online poker where you use cryptocurrencies as payment. Instead of depositing euros or dollars via bank transfer or credit card, you deposit Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum, or other crypto assets.

Prepare Your Crypto Wallet

Before playing, you need a crypto wallet to store and transfer your funds. There are several types of wallets:

  • Hot wallets: Software connected to the internet (Exodus, MetaMask). Convenient for frequent transactions.
  • Cold wallets: Physical devices (Ledger, Trezor). More secure for long-term storage.
  • Exchange wallets: Wallets provided by platforms like Binance or Coinbase. Convenient but less secure.

For playing crypto poker, a hot wallet or exchange wallet is more than enough. Never deposit more than you're willing to lose.

Tips for Beginners

Here are some tips to get started on your crypto poker journey:

  • Manage your bankroll: Never play with money you can't afford to lose. A poker bankroll is an entertainment budget, not an investment.
  • Start small: Micro-stakes are perfect for learning. Don't rush to high-stakes tables.
  • Learn the basics: Know the probabilities, positions, starting hands. Poker is a game of skill.
